Deciding who plays next shouldn't be the hardest part of your session.

For anyone who plays badminton, pickleball, tennis or any court sport with a group of friends, organising a fair rotation is a familiar frustration. Who played last time? Is everyone getting equal court time? Who's sitting out?

Kourt takes care of it. Enter your players, choose your number of courts, and Kourt generates a fair match rotation automatically - so you can spend less time organising and more time playing.

Kourt supports singles and doubles across up to 10 courts at once, with an at-a-glance view of who's playing on each court and a full match history to look back on. Live Activity support means the current matches are visible directly on your lock screen or Always On Display without even unlocking your phone - ideal for keeping track mid-session.

Kourt works completely offline with no account required and no data collected. Everything stays on your device.
